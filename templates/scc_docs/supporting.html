These are all of the SCC contracts for which we have supporting documents. One can see the identifying information on the two PDF files found on the Procurement Department's Active Contracts page, but the only way to get more information about any contract is to file a California Public Records Act request with the Department. One can do this by sending an email to cpra@prc.sccgov.org. I have been successful in my requests by including the Vendor Name, Contract Value, Effective and Expiry Dates, and whichever of the Ariba ID, the Contract ID, and the SAP ID are defined for the contract.

The "Months" column below lists the first and last time that the contract appeared in the reports exported from the Procurement Department. This page, unlike most others found here, includes contracts that are no longer current and which will not be findable in the Procurement Department reports.
